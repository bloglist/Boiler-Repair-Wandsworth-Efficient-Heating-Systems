Common Boiler Issues Faced by Homeowners

  1. Reduced Efficiency: As boilers age, their efficiency tends to decrease. A decline in efficiency leads to higher energy consumption and increased utility bills. Rowlen’s skilled technicians can assess your boiler’s efficiency and recommend appropriate repairs or adjustments to optimize its performance.
  2. Noisy Operation: Strange noises such as banging, gurgling, or whistling coming from your boiler can indicate underlying issues. These noises may be caused by trapped air, pump problems, or faulty components. With their extensive experience, Rowlen’s technicians can identify and rectify these problems, restoring your boiler’s smooth and quiet operation.
  3. Fluctuating Pressure: Fluctuations in boiler pressure can disrupt its functioning and result in inadequate heating or water supply. Rowlen’s experts can diagnose pressure-related issues, such as leaks or faulty pressure valves, and perform the necessary repairs to maintain optimal pressure levels.
  4. Pilot Light Troubles: A malfunctioning pilot light can prevent your boiler from igniting, leaving you without heat. Rowlen’s technicians are well-versed in troubleshooting pilot light issues and can quickly restore its proper functioning, ensuring uninterrupted heating for your home.
  5. Leaking Boilers: Water leaks from a boiler can be a cause for concern, as they can damage surrounding structures and pose safety risks. Rowlen’s professionals possess the expertise to identify the source of the leak and promptly address the issue, preventing further damage and ensuring your safety.
